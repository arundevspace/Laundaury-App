# Process Flows for Laundry Management System

## Overview
This document outlines the various process flows within the Laundry Management System, detailing the interactions between different modules and the steps involved in each process.

## 1. Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Flow
- **Step 1:** Customer Registration
  - Customer submits registration form.
  - System validates and stores customer information.
  
- **Step 2:** Customer Interaction
  - Customer contacts support.
  - Support logs interaction in the system.

- **Step 3:** Customer Updates
  - Customer requests updates to their profile.
  - System processes and updates customer information.

## 2. Order Management Flow
- **Step 1:** Order Creation
  - Customer places an order through the application.
  - System validates order details and creates a new order record.

- **Step 2:** Order Confirmation
  - System sends confirmation to the customer.
  - Order status is updated to "Confirmed".

- **Step 3:** Order Tracking
  - Customer can track the status of their order in real-time.

## 3. Pickup Flow
- **Step 1:** Schedule Pickup
  - Customer requests a pickup for their order.
  - System assigns a pickup agent and schedules the pickup.

- **Step 2:** Pickup Execution
  - Pickup agent collects the order from the customer.
  - System updates the order status to "Picked Up".

## 4. Delivery Flow
- **Step 1:** Schedule Delivery
  - System schedules delivery after processing is complete.
  - Delivery agent is assigned to the task.

- **Step 2:** Delivery Execution
  - Delivery agent delivers the order to the customer.
  - System updates the order status to "Delivered".

## 5. Processing Flow
- **Step 1:** Order Processing
  - Order is received in the processing department.
  - System tracks the processing stages and updates status.

- **Step 2:** Quality Check
  - Processed items undergo a quality check.
  - System logs results and updates item status.

## 6. Inventory Management Flow
- **Step 1:** Stock Update
  - Inventory is updated based on incoming and outgoing stock.
  - System tracks stock levels and movements.

- **Step 2:** Inventory Audit
  - Regular audits are conducted to ensure stock accuracy.
  - System generates reports on stock levels.

## 7. Sales Flow
- **Step 1:** Invoice Generation
  - Upon order completion, an invoice is generated.
  - System sends invoice to the customer.

- **Step 2:** Payment Processing
  - Customer makes payment through the application.
  - System updates payment status and records transaction.

## Conclusion
These process flows provide a comprehensive overview of the interactions and steps involved in the Laundry Management System, ensuring a seamless experience for both customers and staff.
